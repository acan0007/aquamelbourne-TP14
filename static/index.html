Our Mission

We aim to balance urban development with environmental preservation, ensuring that Melbourne's natural resources are protected for future generations. Our goal is to provide urban planners, environmental advocates, and the community with the tools and knowledge they need to make informed decisions and take action towards sustainable water management practices.

Services

Wetland Conservation Awareness

Offer educational resources and interactive tools to help residents learn about wetland ecosystems and participate in their conservation.

Water Quality
Monitoring

A digital platform that monitors water quality in Melbourne's stormwater pits and wetlands in real-time.

Data-Driven Insights

Provide data analytics and reporting tools to help urban planners, environmental organizations, and local government agencies make informed decisions regarding stormwater management and wetland conservation.

Real-Time Water Quality Monitoring

A dedicated section where users can view real-time water quality data of the River. This could include parameters like pH levels, turbidity, dissolved oxygen, and pollutant concentrations.

Nitrogen Pollution

Nitrogen is an essential element in the environment, but its excessive use in agriculture and urban runoff is causing harmful impacts on water quality. Explore interactive maps and learn more about nitrogen levels in everyday products.
