Real-Time Water Quality Monitoring

A dedicated section where users can view real-time water quality data

Below is the map showing real-time water quality data:

Site Name:
The specific location where water quality is being monitored (e.g., Kew, Warrandyte, Healesville, Launching Place).

E. coli (orgs / 100ml):
This indicates the concentration of Escherichia coli bacteria in the water, measured in organisms per 100 milliliters of water.
E. coli is commonly used as an indicator of water contamination, particularly from fecal matter. Higher values suggest potential pollution.

PoPChange (Percentage Change):
This represents the change in E. coli levels compared to the previous reporting period.
It indicates whether the E. coli concentration has increased (positive value) or decreased (negative value) since the last measurement.

Example:
Kew: A decrease of 150 units.
Warrandyte: A decrease of 43 units.
Launching Place: An increase of 64 units.

Water Quality Level:
This gives a qualitative assessment of water quality, typically based on E. coli concentrations or other factors.
In the image, all locations show "Good water quality," which suggests the water is safe for recreational use or other activities.

Below is the bar chart displaying the pollutant levels in the water:

The chart presents data on pollutant levels in the water at different sites over various quarters.
Each panel represents a specific site, and two key measures are displayed:

1. Avg. Level: This likely represents the average level of pollutants at each site, shown by the green bars.
The scale for this measure appears on the left side of each panel, with a range from 0 to 3.

2. Avg. Value: This is represented by the yellow line across each panel. This measure is plotted against the scale
on the right side of each panel, which goes from 0 to 6,000.
This could represent a different dimension of water quality or a specific type of pollutant measured in terms of quantity or concentration (such as micrograms per liter).

The X-axis labeled "Quarter of Date" spans from Q2 2013 to Q3 2024, indicating the time frame for the data collection, segmented into quarters of the year.

Healesville, Kew, Launching Place, and Warrandyte: These are the names of the locations where data was collected. Each panel corresponds to one of these sites, allowing for a comparison of pollutant levels over time and between different locations.

The data visualization helps in tracking the fluctuation of pollution levels over time at these sites, identifying any trends or spikes in pollution, which could be crucial for environmental monitoring and regulatory measures.
